## Source

Matt McGarry — "5 Frameworks That Made My Clients $100M+ From Email" (YouTube + slide deck).

## Key takeaways

- **5 named frameworks**: MAGIC (social to email), Post-signup survey, WEAC (welcome email), Deliverability detox / BSS, Newsletter flywheel / ADAPT. All extracted to `../tactics.md`.
- **Non-obvious wins**: (1) "lazy lead magnets" — never build from scratch, repurpose past content. (2) Linktree in bio kills conversion to <10%, single-link sends 50%+. (3) Ask for one-word replies, never open-ended, in welcome email. (4) DON'T use double opt-in unless legally required — loses 30%+.
- **Biggest surprise**: McGarry has NEVER made a dedicated lead magnet despite driving 10K+ subs from them. All repurposed past newsletters.
